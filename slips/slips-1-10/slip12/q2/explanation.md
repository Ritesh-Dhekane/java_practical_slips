# Slip 12 – Q2: The `final` Keyword

## What the program does
Demonstrates three uses of the `final` keyword:
1. **final variable** – `MAX = 50`; value cannot be reassigned
2. **final class** – `Constants` cannot be extended (subclassed)
3. **final method** – `computeCircleArea()` in `Shape` cannot be overridden

## Java Concepts Used
- **final variable** – constant value
- **final class** – prevents inheritance
- **final method** – prevents overriding in subclass
- Contrast with non-final: `display()` is successfully overridden in `Circle`
