=== Method Overloading – Calculator.add() ===

add(10, 20)           = 30
add(10, 20, 30)        = 60
add(5.5, 3.2)          = 8.7
add(10, 3.14)          = 13.14
add("Hello", " World") = Hello World

Same method name 'add', different parameters – that is Method Overloading.
Java selects the correct version at COMPILE time (static polymorphism).
