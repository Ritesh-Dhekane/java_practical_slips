=== Thread Priority Demo ===

Priority Constants:
  MIN_PRIORITY  = 1
  NORM_PRIORITY = 5
  MAX_PRIORITY  = 10

Thread: HighPriority   | Priority: 10 | State: RUNNABLE
  [HighPriority] step 1
  [HighPriority] step 2
  [HighPriority] step 3
  [HighPriority] finished.
Thread: NormalPriority | Priority: 5  | State: RUNNABLE
  [NormalPriority] step 1
  [NormalPriority] step 2
  [NormalPriority] step 3
  [NormalPriority] finished.
Thread: LowPriority    | Priority: 1  | State: RUNNABLE
  [LowPriority] step 1
  [LowPriority] step 2
  [LowPriority] step 3
  [LowPriority] finished.

All threads completed.
Note: High priority thread gets more CPU time but order is JVM-dependent.
