=== 1. Single Inheritance ===
Animal eats food.
Dog barks.

=== 2. Multilevel Inheritance ===
GrandParent: grandMethod()
Parent: parentMethod()
Child: childMethod()

=== 3. Hierarchical Inheritance ===
Vehicle runs on fuel.
Car: drive()
Vehicle runs on fuel.
Bike: ride()

Note: Java does NOT support Multiple Inheritance via classes.
      Use interfaces to achieve multiple inheritance.
