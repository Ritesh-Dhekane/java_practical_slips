=== Checked vs Unchecked Exceptions ===

--- Checked Exception (IOException) ---
Caught IOException: nonexistent.txt (The system cannot find the file specified)
IOException is CHECKED – compiler forces you to handle it.

--- Unchecked Exception (ArithmeticException) ---
Caught ArithmeticException: / by zero
ArithmeticException is UNCHECKED – compiler does NOT force handling.

--- Unchecked Exception (NullPointerException) ---
Caught NullPointerException: Cannot invoke "String.length()" because "s" is null

--- Unchecked Exception (ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException) ---
Caught ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException: Index 10 out of bounds for length 3

Summary:
CHECKED   – IOException, SQLException, FileNotFoundException
UNCHECKED – ArithmeticException, NullPointerException, ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException
